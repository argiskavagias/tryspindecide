7 Fun Party Games You Can Play Online or at Home with a Custom Spin Wheel

By SpinDecide Editorial • 5 min read

Whether you are hosting a physical house party or hanging out with friends on Zoom, Discord, or Skype, keeping the energy high is crucial. Traditional board games can be slow, while conversation can sometimes drop into awkward silence. That is where a custom choice wheel shines.

By using SpinDecide to create custom wheels, you can turn your screen-share or living room television into a live, interactive game show. Here are 7 creative party games you can play with a custom spin wheel.

1. Custom Truth or Dare (With a Spin)

Instead of playing standard Truth or Dare, load player names on one wheel and difficulty categories or custom prompts on another. Spin the first wheel to select the player, and then spin the second wheel to decide their fate! You can use our built-in Truth or Dare Wheel which has pre-made Friendly, Party, and Spicy (18+) prompts, or load your own inside the settings panel.

2. Trivia Category Selector

Host a trivia night where the subjects are randomized by the wheel. Input categories like *Pop Culture, Geography, History, Movies, Science,* and *Wildcard*. The active player spins the wheel to select their topic. Landing on the "Wildcard" or a bonus segment adds a layer of suspense to the scoreboard.

3. The "Icebreaker" Spin

Great for company team-building events or gatherings where people do not know each other well. Enter interesting discussion questions on the wheel, such as:

  • "What is your most embarrassing childhood memory?"
  • "If you had to live in another historical era, which would it be?"
  • "What is the worst advice you have ever followed?"

Spin the wheel and let each guest take a turn answering. It is an easy way to get people laughing and talking.

4. Impromptu Storytelling Challenge

Put 8-10 random keywords or objects on the wheel (e.g., *Spaceship, Umbrella, Pizza, Haunted House, Talking Cat*). The first player spins the wheel and must begin telling a story incorporating the selected word. The next player spins, gets a new word, and must continue the story logically. It is a fantastic game for developing creativity and improv skills.

Conclusion

A custom choice wheel is a highly versatile tool that can elevate any group gathering. By combining simple rules with the visual and audio satisfaction of a spinning neon wheel, you can create a memorable game night that your friends will talk about for weeks!
